## 1. Securing to Get Certified True Copy of Tax Declaration, Certificate of No Improvement, Aggregate or Land Holding, with Shanty, No Property / No Land Holding

- **Classification:** Complex
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** Tax Payers

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Previous Tax Receipt → Client
2. Tax Declaration → Client
3. Photocopy/copy of land title, write the name of owner and location of the property → Client
4. Barangay Clearance (for hospital and DSWD requirement) → respective Barangay
5. Picture of Property (colored, 3R; for no improvement) → Client

**Fee:** ₱150.00 per requested copy/ies
**Processing time (total):** 3 days, 9 minutes _(verify)_

**Process summary:**

- Fill up the request form at the Office of the City Assessor (~1 minute)
- Proceed to Miscellaneous Division for payment of processing fee (~3–5 minutes; ₱150.00 per copy)
- Return to Counters 5, 6 & 7 for releasing: Certificate of No Improvement — 2 to 3 days; Land Holdings (no property) — 3 minutes
- Bring picture of property (colored, 3R) for inspection if applicable

## 4. For Transfer and Segregation of Tax Declaration

- **Classification:** Simple _(verify)_
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** Tax Payers

**Requirements → where to secure**

_For Transfer of Tax Declaration:_

1. Title (New Owner) — Certified True Copy → Assessor's Office
2. Deed of Sale / Deed of Donation / Deed of Exchange / Extra-Judicial with Publication / Certificate of Consolidation → Client / relevant agency
3. Current Realty Tax and Tax Clearance → Client / Treasurer's Office
4. Capital Gains Tax Receipt (CAR) → BIR
5. Transfer Tax Receipt with Confirmation / Certificate of Payment → Client / Treasurer's Office
6. Garbage Fee Receipt → Client
7. Picture of property (colored, 3R size) → Client
8. Presenter's ID and Phone No. → Client

_For Segregation of Lot:_

1. Title (New Owner) — Certified photocopy → Assessor's Office
2. Segregation Plan → Client / Geodetic Engineer
3. Technical Description → Client / Geodetic Engineer

_For Tax Declaration (National Housing Authority) — New:_

1. Certified true copy of title → NHA / Assessor's Office
2. Awardee Certificate → NHA
3. Technical Description → NHA
4. Picture of property (3R size) → Client

**Fee:** ₱225.00 (tax declaration fee)
**Processing time (total):** 23 days, 5 minutes _(verify)_

**Process summary:**

- Bring all requirements to the Office of the City Assessor
  - Transfer of Tax Declaration: 2–3 working days
  - Segregation of Lot: 5–10 working days (depends on how many parcels)
  - Tax Declaration (National Housing Authority) — New: 5–10 working days
- Proceed to Miscellaneous Division for payment of processing fee (~3–5 minutes; ₱225.00 tax declaration)
- Return to Assessor's Office for releasing

## Feedback and Complaints Mechanism

- **Send feedback:** Answer client feedback form and drop at designated drop box in front of each counter as City Assessor's Office.
- **How feedbacks are processed:** Every last day of the week, the Public Relation Officer compiles all feedback forms from the drop box. Complaints and feedbacks are forwarded to the concerned offices and they are required to respond within three (3) days upon receipt thereof. For inquiries, the clients or taxpayers may contact the City Assessor's Office at **8874-4781** _(verify)_.
- **How to file complaints:** Citizens of Las Piñas may file their complaints through letters or e-mail addressed to the City Assessor. The complaints are being processed based on existing office procedures and laws. Based on the merit of the complaint, it may be referred to the right forum for appropriate action.
- **Contact Information:**
  - City Assessor's Office Email: cityassessor.lc@gmail.com
  - ARTA Email: complaints@arta.gov.ph
  - Contact Center ng Bayan (CCB) Email: email@contactcenterngbayan.gov.ph
  - Presidential Complaint Center Email: pcc@malacanang.gov.ph
