## 1. Receiving of Official Communications, Complaints and Appointments with the Mayor

- **Classification:** Simple
- **Type of transaction:** G2C and G2G
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Letter / Communication / Appointment / other Document/s → Client

**Fee:** None
**Processing time (total):** 2 days, 7 minutes _(verify)_

---

## 2. Endorsement of Walk-in Client

- **Classification:** Simple
- **Type of transaction:** G2C and G2G
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Request / Complaint (verbal) → Client

**Fee:** None
**Processing time (total):** 2 days, 4 minutes _(verify)_

---

## 3. Issuance of Financial and Medical Assistance

- **Classification:** Highly Technical
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** All

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Medical Records / Medical Abstract → Hospital
2. Hospital Bill (Financial Assistance) → Hospital
3. Request Letter (indicate the purpose; for Financial or Medical Assistance) → the City Mayor
4. Barangay Clearance (indicate the purpose) → respective Barangay
5. Photocopy of ID (patient and claimant) → Client

**Fee:** None
**Processing time (total):** 2 weeks, 4 minutes _(verify)_

---

## 4. Mayor's Clearance

- **Classification:** Complex
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Barangay Clearance → respective Barangay
2. Police Clearance → online (pnpclearance.ph) or City Police Station
3. Payment Receipt of Mayor's Clearance → Local Treasury Office

**Fee:** ₱75.00 _(verify)_
**Processing time (total):** 3 days, 18 minutes _(verify)_

---

## 5. Appointment with the Mayor

- **Classification:** Simple to Complex
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Request Letter for Appointment → Client

**Fee:** None
**Processing time:** Depends upon the availability of the Mayor's schedule _(verify)_

- **Send feedback:** Accomplish feedback form, drop at the box in front of the City Public Relations & Information Office, or email laspinascitypio@yahoo.com _(verify)_.
- **Complaints:** Accomplish Complaints Form (name of person being complained, incident, evidence). Inquiries/follow-up via telephone **8-871-4343** or **8-871-4345**.
- **Contact (CCB / PCC / ARTA):** CCB SMS 0908-881-6565 / 1-6565 (landline); PCC pcc@malacanang.gov.ph (+632) 8736-8645 / 8736-8621; ARTA complaints@arta.gov.ph _(verify all numbers)_.
