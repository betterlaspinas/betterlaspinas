## 1. One-Stop Shop for Construction Permit

- **Classification:** Simple
- **Type of transaction:** G2B and G2G — Government to Citizen / Government to Government
- **Who may avail:** Private Individual Firm or Corporation Government Agency

**Requirements → where to secure**

_You may download, edit, and print application forms and checklist of requirements thru: https://bit.ly/lpcengineeringforms_

**Single Dwelling / Residential / Commercial / Industrial / Others**
_(For simple structures — 5,000 sq. m. & below)_

1. Four (4) copies of filled up Application Forms for Building Permit and its Ancillary Permits → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section
2. Application Form for Locational Clearance → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section
3. Fire Safety Evaluation Clearance → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section
4. Latest Tax Clearance/Tax Declaration → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section
5. Certified True Electronics copy of Original Certificate of Title (OCT) / Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT), or Notarized Deed of Absolute Sale or Lot Locational Plan from LRA if lessee; or Contract of Lease → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section
6. Four (4) sets of Relocation Plans and Survey Certificates/Reports → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section
7. Design Plans and other Documents:
   - 7.1 Architectural Documents with Provisions for BP 344 (Accessibility Law) if applicable
   - 7.2 Civil/Structural Design Analysis Computation with Seismic Analysis (for 7.5m & above), Soil Test (for three (3) storeys and higher)
   - Electrical Documents → Office of the City Engineer/Building Official – Building Permit Section

**Fee:** Varies — per PD 1096: Processing fee ~₱750.00, Filing fee ~₱300.00 _(verify)_
**Processing time (total):** 1 hour and 8 minutes

## Feedback and Complaints Mechanism

- **How to send feedback:** Accomplish client feedback form and drop it at designated drop box in front of each counter as City Engineering Office.
- **How feedbacks are processed:** Complaints and feedbacks are forwarded to the designated offices and they are required to respond within three (3) days upon the receipt thereof. For inquiries, the clients may contact the City Engineering Office at **8873-3004**.
- **How to file complaints:** Citizen of Las Piñas may file their complaints through letters or e-mail addressed to the Office of the City Engineer. The complaints are being processed based on existing office procedures and laws. Based on the merit of the complaint, it may be referred to the right forum for appropriate action.
- **Contact Information:**
  - Office of the City Engineer — Contact No.: **8873-3004**; Email: lbdpo@laspinas.gov.ph _(verify)_
  - ARTA — Email: complaints@arta.gov.ph
  - Contact Center ng Bayan (CCB) — Email: email@contactcenternqbayan.gov.ph _(verify)_
  - Presidential Complaint Center — Email: pcc@malacanang.gov.ph _(verify)_
  - Citizens' Complaint Center — Contact No.: **8888**
