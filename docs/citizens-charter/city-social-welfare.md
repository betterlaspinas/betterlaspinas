## 1. Medical Assistance / Burial/Financial Assistance / Free Cremation / Financial Assistance

- **Classification:** Complex
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

_For Medical Assistance_

1. Personal Letter Address to City Mayor → Client
2. Medical Certificate with license/PTR No. of the attending physician → Hospital where the client is confined
3. Barangay Certificate → Client
4. Prescription (for medicine) with price → Client's Physician
5. Personal letter address to City Mayor → Client
6. 2 valid ID of the patient or his/her representative (e.g. driver's license, postal, school, voters and senior citizen ID) → Government Agencies

_For Burial/Financial Assistance_

1. Certified Photocopy of Death Certificate (death of a family member) → Civil Registry
2. Barangay Certification (claimant) → Respective Barangay
3. Funeral Contract (claimant) → Funeral Parlor
4. 2 valid ID of the patient or his/her representative (e.g. driver's license, postal, school, voters and senior citizen ID) → Government Agencies

_For Free Cremation_

1. Certified Photocopy of Death Certificate → Civil Registry
2. Funeral Contract → Funeral Parlor
3. Barangay Certificate (claimant) → Respective Barangay
4. Notarized Sworn Statement → Client

_For Financial Assistance (Assistive Device)_

1. Personal Letter address to City Mayor → Client
2. Barangay Certification (claimant) → Claimant
3. Any Valid Identification Card → Respective Barangay / Government Agencies
4. Picture of Patient → Client

_For Financial Assistance (Livelihood)_

1. Personal Letter Address to City Mayor → Client
2. Barangay Certificate → Claimant
3. 2 Valid ID of the client → Respective Barangay / Government Agencies

**Fee:** None
**Processing time (total):** 5 days and 20 minutes _(verify)_

## 2. Issuance of Social Case Study Report

- **Classification:** Simple
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Endorsement/Acceptance Letter coming from hospital → Hospital Concern
2. Original and latest Medical Certificate / Clinical Abstract → Hospital Concern
3. Final and Latest Barangay Certificate → Hospital Concern
4. Hospital Bill / Quotation of said chemo / Prescribed Medicines → Hospital Concern
5. Assessor's Certificate (if no existing property) → Local Assessor's Office

**Fee:** None
**Processing time (total):** 30 minutes _(verify)_

## 5. Provide Disaster Relief Assistance

- **Classification:** Simple
- **Type of transaction:** G2C
- **Who may avail:** General Public

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Barangay Certificate of Residency → Respective Barangay
2. Barangay Certification indicating that the client is affected by the disaster → Respective Barangay
3. Police blotter or Certificate from the Bureau of Fire Protection → Police Precinct / Fire Bureau
4. Pictures → Claimant

**Fee:** None
**Processing time (total):** 10 Minutes _(verify)_
