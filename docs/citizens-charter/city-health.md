- **Who may avail:** Bonafide Residents of Las Piñas City

**Requirements → where to secure**

1. Covid 19 vaccination card → Vaccination site
2. Xerox copy of local travel ticket → Client
3. Copy of S Pass → Client
4. Xerox copy of valid ID → Client

**Client Steps**

| #   | Client Steps                                                                      | Agency Actions                                                                   | Fee          | Processing Time                  | Person Responsible   |

| 2   | Give to Staff on duty the documents required for Vaccination Certificate.         | Receive and Process documents submitted.                                         | LIBRE / FREE | 5 to 10 minuto / 5 to 10 minutes | Office staff on duty |
